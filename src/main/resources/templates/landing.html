Platform Features

Everything your organisation needs, under one roof

From onboarding your first member to hosting your annual general meeting — Clubknit handles every layer of your organisation's digital life.

Member & Group Management

Maintain a structured registry of members across multiple clubs and sub-groups. Control access with built-in approval workflows and granular role-based permissions.

Real-Time Messaging & Channels

Dedicated channels for every club, group and project. Conversations stay organised, searchable and fully accessible across desktop and mobile.

Project Management

Plan, assign and monitor projects with full clarity. Supports both client-facing and internal projects, with visibility across all teams and stakeholders.

Invoicing, Dues & Donations

Automate membership subscriptions, event payments and donation collection. Generate professional invoices and track all financial activity in one place.

Video Calls & Town Halls

Host virtual meetings, AGMs and open town halls directly within the platform. No third-party tool required — bring every member into the room.

AI-Powered Support

An intelligent support assistant helps members get answers instantly. Raise tickets, receive resolutions and escalate issues — all within the platform, powered by AI.

Certificates, Modules & Coaching

Issue and manage member certificates, run structured learning modules and coordinate coaching programmes — all within a single integrated environment.

Gallery & Media Management

Showcase events, milestones and club achievements in a beautifully organised media gallery — shareable across web and mobile in just a few clicks.

Network & Member Connections

Help members build meaningful connections within your organisation. Peer profiles, introductions and collaboration opportunities — all in one secure network.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes, not months

Getting your club or association onto Clubknit is straightforward — no technical knowledge required, no lengthy setup process.

1

Your organisation is set up

An administrator registers the organisation and configures its clubs, groups, member roles and permissions on the platform — typically in under an hour.

2

Members are invited securely

Members sign in through your organisation's dedicated portal, authenticated via Keycloak SSO — no extra accounts to create, no passwords to juggle.

3

Collaboration happens in real time

Members join channels, participate in sub-groups, attend virtual town halls, track projects and contribute to the club — from any device, anywhere.

4

Admins stay fully in control

Organisation and project administrators have dedicated access portals giving full visibility into membership, financials, approvals and platform activity.
